Frequently Asked Questions

What is a row?

A row is a single row of data as you would find in a CSV or spreadsheet. If you have multiple tables, we will join the corresponding fields to create a concatenated row. You can see the rows that you have consumed in the Settings panel of your account.

What if I do not have a model?

Currently, Apres supports model production in the Enterprise package. Our team of AI Solutions engineers will work with your team to understand your data, define the model and import it into Apres. After the model is imported, the platform will work to improve the model, as it would with any native model client. All model assets are available for download in the platform.

What data is required for Apres to work?

Apres requires that you integrate your raw model data and model predictions. When testing the system, a representative sample of data is required - up to 10% of the total training data. Our system will automatically create a Surrogate Model to match your model's behavior as well as challenger models that prove the efficacy of the features developed on the platform.

How do you deal with security?

Our platform will deploy a single-tenant instance for every account. This means that your data and/or insights will never commingle with other accounts. Beyond your federated account, you can expect that Apres deals strictly with data security and follows GDPR regulations.
